Steps needed to prepare the Maggie

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger garlic. Saute on high flame for a minute and add spring onions, carrots and capsicum. Stir fry for a minute and add soy sauce and ketchup.
  2. Add the maggi noodles and toss well. Cook for a minute and switch off the flame. Serve immediately.
